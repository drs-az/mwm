FAQ

Short answers for Activities Directors & operators.

Do residents need singing experience?

No. It’s designed for all ability levels. Humming and lyric‑reading are welcome too.

What room/equipment is needed?

Chairs in a semi‑circle and a quiet space. We bring lyric packets and optional speaker for light accompaniment.

Can we include assisted living or memory care?

Yes—there’s an optional same‑day Memory‑Care Melodies add‑on (30–40 min) tailored for comfort and recall.

Do you carry insurance?

Yes. We can name your community as an additional insured on our general liability policy.
